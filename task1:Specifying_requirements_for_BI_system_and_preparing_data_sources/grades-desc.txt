Grades CSV 

(Information about grades of the students for tests, line 1 is a header row) 

COLUMN 1 (student_id) - Student identification number (unique numeric value, 8 digits, used also in the database) 

COLUMN 2 (test_id) - Test identification number (unique numeric value, 8 digits, used also in the database) 

COLUMN 3 (grade) - Grade obtained by the student (value from 1 to 10) 

COLUMN 4 (percentage) – Percentage score obtained by the student (value from 0 to 100) 

COLUMN 5 (writing_time) – Writing time (value in percentage from 0 to 100) 

COLUMN 6 (attempt_num) – Attempt number (1 or 2)
